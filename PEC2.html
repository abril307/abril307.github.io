VERLBA VIOLENCE 
WHAT IS VERBAL VIOLENCE?
 
Verbal abuse occurs when one person 
criticizes or insults another person.
 It is a destructive form of communication 
intended to damage the other person's 
self-concept and produce negative emotions.
HOW TO AVOID VERBAL VIOLENCE 
Assertive communication and communication skills in general reduce the risk of 
conflict and verbal violence. Self-esteem, empathy, concern for others or the 
ability to manage conflicts constructively contribute to reducing the risk of using 
verbal violence.
